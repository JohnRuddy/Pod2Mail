About Us

Welcome to Pod2Mail! Our platform allows you to summarize your favorite podcast episodes and receive them conveniently via email in .docx format.

With Pod2Mail, you can:

  • Input a podcast RSS feed URL
  • View the latest 10 episodes
  • Select up to 3 episodes for summarization
  • Receive AI-generated summaries directly in your inbox

We aim to make podcast content more accessible and easy to consume. Thank you for using Pod2Mail!
